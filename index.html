Welcome to Sustainability Insights

A reposistory of my small but non trivial projects to make our way of life more sustainable and the insights gained by doing the same.

Most of the technology used today was designed to work on fossil fuels. This however, has to change for the better by being sustainable. Having an avid interest in Physics that later morphed into an interest in Engineering, I want to contribute to this so called 'revolution' by helping build technology for a greener and more prosperous future.

Electrical and Electronics engineering is my immediate area of interest as the inner functions of the machines we use today could be made more sustainable in various ways. This means making today's tech more efficient, economically viable and powered by green energy. This coupled with Computer Science, something that does things at a scale and rate that humans simply can't match, would help revolutionize world and I want to be a part of it.

I find Julia as a versatile language for performing data analysis and for use in scientific computing. All work presented here uses Julia, unless stated otherwise. Julia is fast, making it one of the most efficient languages for a host of functions.

Analysis of e-Waste buildup

eCommerce purchase history datasets from Kaggle and data crawled from product descriptions have been used for this analysis.

Julia source code for this analysis is available as Open Source here : eWaste analysis source code

Insights:

  • Smartphones is the largest category by volume and value
  • Smartphones' batteries are usually Li-ion, manufacturing of which has substantial environment impact

Further reading:

  • Economic factors governing use of batteries
  • Responsible Battery Coalition of companies and academia committed to the responsible management of the batteries
